F.A.Q.

How to shop?

If you aren't from Japan, you can use a proxy like Buyee, Neokyo, Zen Market or Rakufun to shop from Japanese brands from your preference.

What is a proxy?

It's a service that acts as a personal shopper and package forwarder for international customers, allowing them to buy items from Japanese stores that don't ship overseas.

Which are the most popular styles?

A lot of styles are popular between the J-fashion community, but Gyaru and Jirai are the most popular recently.

Where can I find out more about J-fashion styles?

You can search on wikis that are dedicated on collecting all the styles and their info.
